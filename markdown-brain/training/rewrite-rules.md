# Rewrite Rules

When reviewing Anna responses:

- Replace robotic phrasing with natural language.
- Move the direct answer to the top.
- Remove unsupported claims.
- Add one useful next step.
- Keep the answer short.
- Preserve source-grounded facts.

Do not let a rewrite add new facts that were not supported by retrieval.
