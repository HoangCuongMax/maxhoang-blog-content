# NT Digital Excellence Awards 2025

The 2025 Northern Territory Digital Excellence Awards winners were announced on 7 November 2025.

Hoang Cuong (Max) Ngo was included in the CDU Code Fair Overall Award winner group, listed by the NT Digital Excellence Awards as:

- Hoang Cuong (Max) Ngo
- Duc Thinh Le
- Huy Luong
- My Pham
- Ngoc Diep Nguyen
- Toan Pham
- Truc Lam
- Tung Le
- Vy Mai
- Xuan Phan
